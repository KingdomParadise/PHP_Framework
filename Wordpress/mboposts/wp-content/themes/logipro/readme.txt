Create a world-class transport business website with a responsive design packed full of useful features, built to top standards, e-commerce ready & 6 months support.

LogiPro is a fully responsive WordPress theme that allows you to build a professional-looking website for your transportation company.

Crafted with care, this logipro template comes with a clean code and a great documentation. An attractive About page will help you to show up your values, pricing, history, and other key aspects in the best light, while pre-loaded testimonials and team members modules will help to make your cargo company far more reliable for your clients.

A wide range of additional pages allows you to present your transportation business in little things, meanwhile a rich UI kit will help to fill up your website with multiple types of content. A captivating design, social options, Google maps integration, multiple color options, and lots of other features will help you to promote your shipping company all over the web!

Enjoy codeless website building with Visual Composer page builder and edit your theme up to your taste with WordPress Live Customizer. Lead in logipro business with this warehousing theme!

Please visit; https://doc.freevision.me/logistics for documentation and further help.
